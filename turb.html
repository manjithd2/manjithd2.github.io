TURB

Public Transport tracking inside campus

Going to classes from the hostel zone in a humongous campus like mine is a herculean tasks. Recently they had introduced bus and electric car services to solve this problem but they didn't know that they had given rise to another one.

THE PROBLEM

Buses were given a schedule to follow, to stop at the main places at the alloted times. But what if you were in the middle of two stops and wanted to catch a bus? What if the bus was late? You would want to know the location of the bus. Electric cars operated the same way, shuttling between stops but they weren't given a schedule to follow, giving them all the more reason to be tracked and be used to their fullest.

SOLUTION

Since I wasn't the only one facing the problem of lack of information, I decided why not make an app to solve it. The user would just have to click the app which would lead to a map like interface with the location of the buses and the electric cars. Since there are many buses following different routes, clicking on one bus would show it's details with it's route and estimated time of arrival so you know how much to wait.

But, how will the tracking take place?

The tracking would take place when the driver opens the driver app and enters his vehicle details
